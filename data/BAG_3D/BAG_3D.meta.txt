Beschrijving:

The Amsterdam BAG-3D dataset is based on two sources:
- Function, area and year of construction of buildings from Basisregistratie Adressen en Gebouwen (BAG), maintained and published by The Netherlands’ Cadastre, Land Registry and Mapping Agency – in short Kadaster.
- Height of the building from Actueel Hoogtebestand Nederland (AHN), maintained and published by Waterschappen, Rijkswaterstaat en Provincies.
Both are accessible via https://www.pdok.nl/.
ESRI has made available BAG-3D dataset, which combines BAG and AHN. Maarten van 't Zelfde, Intitute of Environmental Sciences, Leiden Unversity, has extracted from BAG-3D the data for Amsterdam, using ArcGIS 10.2. 

The data are used in the PUMA project, coordinator Ester van der Voet, Intitute of Environmental Sciences, Leiden Unversity. Job Spierings and Ruben Huele are working on a visualisation of the data.
